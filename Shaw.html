Dr. Evelyn Shaw

1926/1927 - May 19, 2003

Dr. Shaw, a writer and biologist, was the daughter of Polish immigrants. She graduated from NYU and Started working in the American Museum of Natural History as a scientist and curator. She researched fish who swim in schools.
